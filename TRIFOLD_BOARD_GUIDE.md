## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Visual Design:
1. **Use a starfield background** - Print a dark space background to cover panels
2. **Color-code planets** - Match colors to website theme
3. **Add borders** - Use colored borders around sections
4. **Icons** - Use emoji or simple icons for visual interest
5. **White space** - Don't overcrowd - leave breathing room

### QR Code Placement:
1. Place at **eye level** (not too high or low)
2. Add **clear labels** ("Scan to explore HD 189733 b")
3. Include **backup text** with website URL
4. **Test visibility** from 3-4 feet away
5. Use **high contrast** (black QR on white background)

### Content Strategy:
1. **Hook readers** with the most extreme fact first
2. **Use numbers** - they catch attention
3. **Keep text brief** - bullet points over paragraphs
4. **Highlight contrasts** - "Darker than coal!" "Two suns!"
5. **Make it interactive** - QR codes encourage engagement

### Layout Tips:
1. **Rule of thirds** - Place key elements at intersections
2. **Left to right flow** - Introduction â†’ Details â†’ Comparisons
3. **Visual hierarchy** - Largest text = most important
4. **Consistent spacing** - Use equal margins throughout
5. **Alignment** - Keep elements lined up
